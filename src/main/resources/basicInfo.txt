





                Welcome to Terminal Velocity, an exhilarating console-based
                murder mystery set aboard the International Space Station (ISS).
                In this gripping tale, you find yourself as an intrepid
                investigator trapped amidst the vast expanses of space,
                facing a heinous crime that threatens the safety of everyone
                aboard. As you venture through the intricate corridors of the ISS,
                uncovering secrets and deciphering clues, it is up to you
                to solve the enigma and unmask the cold-blooded killer.
